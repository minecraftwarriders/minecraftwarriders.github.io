War Riders Coins

One balance for cosmetic unlocks.

Coins are the in-game store currency for cosmetic-only items. They are not power, gear, land, or an advantage. They are just a cleaner way to save up and pick the style you want.

Cosmetic only No P2W Never expires
How coins work

Buy coins here, then spend them in-game on cosmetics like trails, pets, hats, and chat tags. Once your coin order is confirmed, the coins are added to your player automatically.

Browse cosmetics Buy coin bundles

What Coins Buy

Coins buy cosmetic unlocks only. That means style, identity, and fun extras, without changing survival balance.

How To Spend

Open the in-game cosmetic store, pick an unlock, and spend coins from your player balance.

Username Matters

Use your exact Minecraft username when buying coins so the balance goes to the right player.
