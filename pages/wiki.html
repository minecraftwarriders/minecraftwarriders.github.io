Beginner's Guide

Welcome to Minecraft War Riders! This guide will help you get started with the essential commands and systems.

First Time Joining?
When you first join the server, you'll start with basic permissions and $1000 in your account. Use /spawn to teleport to the main hub area.

Essential Commands to Remember

/spawn
Teleport to the server spawn point
/home
Teleport to your home (if set)
/sethome [name]
Set a home at your current location
/shop
Open the main server shop GUI
/balance
Check your current money balance
/skills
Open your skills progression menu
💡 Pro Tip
Use /back after dying to return to your death location and collect your items!
