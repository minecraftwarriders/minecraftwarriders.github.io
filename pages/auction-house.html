Auction House Rules Infographic

  • Setting reasonable starting prices and durations
  • Bidding on multiple auctions simultaneously
  • Canceling auctions with no bids

üö´ Not Allowed

  • Using alternate accounts to inflate bids

Auction Strategies and Tips

üéØ Buying Tips

  • Research item values before bidding
  • Set a maximum bid limit and stick to it
  • Bid in the final minutes for best chances
  • Watch for auctions ending at off-peak times

üí∞ Selling Tips

  • Start auctions when most players are online
  • Set competitive starting prices to attract bidders
  • Use shorter durations for high-demand items
  • Bundle related items for better value

‚è∞ Timing Strategy

  • Weekend evenings see the most activity
  • Avoid starting auctions before server restarts
  • Consider different time zones for international players
  • Holiday events may affect item demand and prices
