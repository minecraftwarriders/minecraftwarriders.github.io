Automated selling system for farms and resource gathering

üìã Overview

AutoSellChests are special chests that automatically sell any items you put inside them. They're perfect for farms, mining operations, and any situation where you want to convert items to money without manually visiting the shop.

üõí How to Get AutoSellChests: AutoSellChests can be bought from the server shop, but CANNOT be sold back. Once you buy one, it's yours to keep!

üí∞ Pricing

  • Buy Price: Available in the shop (see /shop or item prices page)
  • Sell Price: Cannot be sold back to the server
  • Investment: One-time purchase that pays for itself over time

üîß How to Use AutoSellChests

Using AutoSellChests is simple and straightforward:

Purchase: Buy an AutoSellChest from the server shop using /shop
Place: Place the AutoSellChest wherever you want (near farms, mines, etc.)
Add Items: Put any sellable items inside the chest
Automatic Sale: Items are automatically sold and money is added to your balance
üí° Perfect for Farms: Place AutoSellChests at the end of your farms so harvested crops automatically convert to money when you deposit them!

‚öôÔ∏è How It Works

  • Instant Selling: Items are sold immediately when placed in the chest
  • Server Prices: Uses the same prices as the /shop command
  • Only Sellable Items: Items that can't be sold to the shop won't be processed
  • Money Added: Proceeds are automatically added to your balance
  • No Manual Work: No need to manually visit the shop or type commands

‚ö° Commands & Management

AutoSellChests work automatically, but here are some useful related commands:

/shop
Open the shop to purchase AutoSellChests
/balance
Check how much money you've earned from auto-selling
/worth [item]
Check how much an item sells for before putting it in the chest
‚ö†Ô∏è No Undo: Once items are placed in an AutoSellChest, they're immediately sold and cannot be retrieved. Make sure you want to sell items before putting them in!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I get my items back after putting them in?

No - Items are sold immediately and permanently. Double-check before putting valuable items in!

Q: What items can be sold?

Any item that can be sold through /shop can be auto-sold. Use /worth [item] to check.

Q: Can other players use my AutoSellChest?

AutoSellChests work like regular chests for access, but the money goes to the chest owner. Use protection plugins if needed.

Q: Do AutoSellChests work when I'm offline?

AutoSellChests process items when they're accessed, so they work best when you or other players are online.

Q: Can I move my AutoSellChest?

Yes! Break it with any tool and it will drop as an item that you can place elsewhere.
